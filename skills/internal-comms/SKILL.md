# Internal Communications

## When to Use
- Writing status reports
- Team newsletters
- Project announcements
- Meeting recaps
- FAQ documents
- Policy updates

## Best Practices

### Status Reports
- Lead with what matters most
- Use clear sections: Highlights, Blockers, Next Steps
- Keep it concise - bullet points > paragraphs
- Include metrics where possible

### Newsletters
- Catchy but professional subject line
- Organize by topic/category
- Include calls to action
- Add visual variety (headers, dividers)

### Announcements
- Be clear and direct
- Explain the "why" not just the "what"
- Include next steps or timeline
- Set expectations

## Tone Guidelines
- Professional but personable
- Avoid corporate jargon
- Be direct - don't bury the point
- Match company culture

## Format Tips
- Use headers for scannability
- Keep paragraphs short
- Use bullet points for lists
- Include contact info when relevant
